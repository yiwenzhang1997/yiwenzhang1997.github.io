About Me

Hi there, I'm Yiwen!
I'm a 4th year phd student in cognitive psychology, working with Dr. Ben Rottman at Univeristy of Pittsburgh, and a UX researcher intern at Meta with WhatsApp research team during summer 2022. Before that, I received my B.S in Psychology from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. My research interest centers around human causal learning and causal inference. I'm also passionate about using quantitative methods to explore human behavior.
Outside the lab, I enjoy cooking, crocheting, leisure reading, and hanging out with my dog!
